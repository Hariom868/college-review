MODE OF ADMISSION

One needs to take the WBJEE exam or the JEE Main exam.

Admissions for 90% of the seats are done on the basis of rank in WBJEE through WBJEEB Counselling.

Admissions for the remaining 10% of the seats are done on the basis of rank in JEE Main through WBJEEB Counselling.
