MODE OF ADMISSION

  • After clearning BITSAT exams one individual get eligible to take admissions in one of the BITS college.
  • Admissions are done on the basis of score in BITSAT through BITS Counselling.

Scholarship

There are multiple scholarships available at BITS namely Merit Scholarships, Merit Cum Need Scholarships and BITSAA Scholarships. Every year 30% of students admitted to First Degree Programs get between 25% to 100% tuition fee waivers in form of different scholarships.
